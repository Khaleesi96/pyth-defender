HOW PYTH ENTROPY WORKS HERE
1
YOU PLAY THE GAME — Earn a score in Pyth Defender. Live BTC/ETH/SOL/BNB prices from Pyth Hermes feed the game in real-time.
2
SUBMIT ON-CHAIN — Click "Submit Score". Your browser calls submitScore() on the deployed contract, paying a small Pyth Entropy fee (~0.0001 ETH).
3
ENTROPY REQUESTS — The contract calls entropy.requestV2() on Pyth's on-chain Entropy contract (0x4821932D…). A sequenceNumber is issued.
4
PYTH REVEALS — Pyth's off-chain Fortuna service detects the request and calls back entropyCallback() on your contract with a cryptographically secure bytes32 randomNumber.
5
BONUS APPLIED — The random number maps to a bonus tier: 1.0× (50%) · 1.25× (25%) · 1.5× (15%) · 2.0× (8%) · 2.5× LEGENDARY (2%). Final score is stored on-chain — tamper-proof and fully verifiable.
